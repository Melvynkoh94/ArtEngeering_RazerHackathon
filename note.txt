Create an app which can consolidate all the Visa cards from various banks.
All the transactions (shopping, insurance) made with Visa cards can be consolidated.
On top of each credit card/account benefits,
    - every $100 dollar spent will collect one stamp
    - every week there will be 1 game for a chance to win one stamp.
Collect 8 stamps to redeem prizes (shopping vouchers, rebate for financial services paid by Visa cards)

Visa APIs to use
https://developer.visa.com/apibrowser/#segment=Issuers
- To add cards in the app 
    Payment Account Validation https://developer.visa.com/capabilities/pav/docs
- For people to track their spending
    Visa Cardholder Purchase Inquiry https://developer.visa.com/capabilities/visa-cardholder-purchase-inquiry
- 

Use Perx to create rewards vouchers
